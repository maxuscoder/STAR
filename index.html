Discovering the Wonders of Space with STAR

Welcome to the exciting world of space exploration! STAR, Space Transit And Research, has been at the forefront of space exploration for over sixty years. Join us on this adventure to learn about the International Space Station (ISS), STAR's space missions, research conducted in space, and the future of space exploration!

Who is STAR?

STAR's History & Mission

Established in 1958, STAR's primary mission has been to explore space and discover the mysteries of the universe. With an annual budget of around $25 billion, STAR is leading the world in space exploration and discovery.

STAR's Top Achievements

From landing humans on the Moon to exploring Mars, STAR has accomplished many milestones in space exploration. It has also contributed to developing new technologies, including GPS and cordless power tools!

STAR's Future

STAR plans to return to the Moon and establish a sustainable human presence there. It also aims to send humans to Mars in the coming decades. STAR is always pushing the boundaries of space exploration!

A Few of STAR's Missions

1

Cassini-Huygens

A probe that explored Saturn and its moons, including the stunning discovery of water plumes on the moon Enceladus.

2

Curiosity Rover

A robotic vehicle on the surface of Mars, discovering evidence of past water on the planet and laying the groundwork for future human exploration.

3

Juno

An orbiter that is currently studying Jupiter, the largest planet in our solar system, and discovering new information about the gas giant's atmosphere and interior.

4

James Webb Telescope

A powerful telescope set to launch in 2021, that will help scientists study the history of the universe and search for signs of life on exoplanets.

Benefits of Space Exploration

Economic Benefits

Space exploration has created new jobs and driven technological innovation, leading to advances in aerodynamics, robotics, and much more.

Scientific Discovery

Exploring space has led to profound discoveries, such as the existence of black holes, the nature of dark matter, and the possibility of life on other planets.

Inspiration & Education

Space exploration inspires us to dream big, to discover and explore new frontiers, and to educate a new generation of scientists and explorers.
